Gestalt Principles for grouping

    Similarity

  • Our brain uses similarity to distinguish between objects who may lay adjacent to or overlap with each other based upon their visual texture
  • All the contents in gallery page has same background and the CSS Stylesheets have hiden properties to some elements

    • Proximity

  • The Gestalt law of proximity states that "objects or shapes that are close to one another appear to form groups".
  • Images in the gallery followed with text description.
  • Gallery page is well balanced and organized

    • Closure

  • Buttons in the gallery has thumbnails so that the user can have more closure

    • Good Form

  • The principle of good form refers to the tendency to group together forms of similar shape, pattern, color etc.
  • Gallery page content are well grouped
  • Site map is well grouped and has a simple structure.
