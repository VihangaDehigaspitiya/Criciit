Information Page Design

Accessibility

  • The main page is designed in a simple and elegant way that the user wont be confused.
  • I have created two different stylesheets for the navigation bar and main page.
  • Since the navigation bar should be used in every almost every web page its much more easier to have a seperate stylesheet.
  • The navigation bar allowas the user to surf around the website without getting drowned.

  • Forms are usually dull to see.
  • BUt with a little dose of css i was able to give it a interesting look

Typhography

  • Sans-Serif is frequently used in the main page because it gives a classy look with the blend of the reddish background.
  • I thought of having a stylish navigation bar but still having the clarity it has the most suitable fonts were "Lucida Console", "Lucida Sans Typewriter", "Bitstream Vera Sans Mono"
  • Different font sizes were given to diffent sections in order to maintain a clear idea on the website

  • I didn't use many font types in the form in order to make it look more professional
  • The form uses mostly the default font families.

Color Balance / Combination

  • Red and Black was chosen as the theme color since these color's represents a professional look for the website.
  • The background color represents the theme very well, its a black and red gradient.
  • The navigation bar has a hover effect of blue color which also blends well with the theme.
  • I have used white fonts in the nav bar because it looks amazing with and without the hover effect
  • But in the form the background colors are fixed so i settled with black color which is easier to identify.

Gestalt Principles for grouping

    Similarity

  • The textboxes in the form has the same hover effect in order to represent their similarity
  • Also the navigations links share the same properties
  • In the main page same type of contents share the same background

    • Proximity

  • The Gestalt law of proximity states that "objects or shapes that are close to one another appear to form groups".
  • Labels in top of the text boxes
  • Contents are well oragnized according to their types and uses.

    • Closure

  • The form is also developed sepereately where the content can be easily identified.

    • Good Form

  • The principle of good form refers to the tendency to group together forms of similar shape, pattern, color etc.
  • The navigation bar groups the whole site together
