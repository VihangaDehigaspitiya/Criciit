Information Page Design

Accessibility

  • The quiz game was made with a simple and beautiful interface which could be very easy to play by the users.
  • Check marks (Correct and Wrong Marks) were given for every question.
  • Group Members' images were given a black and white to high quality mouse over effects.
  • Group Members' Details were ordered in a proper manner to increase the readability for users.
  • A fixed position is given to the Timer to make it more effective.
  • A “go to Top” button is given to all the pages. So, any page longer than the screen size could be linked to various part of the text at the top of the page.

Typhography

  • Fonts like “Lucida Grande”, “Sans-Serif”, “Comic sans”, “Arial” were used for the Main page and for the Quiz game.
  • Different font sizes and fonts were given to all the sections because it will make each section unique.
  • The line spacing in the Progress section of the Quiz form is much greater and the alignment of the text to the left / right.
  • Matching font colors and background colors were given to group members’ details.
  • Using “Comic sans” font as the main font for the Quiz game gives a classy beautiful look.

Color Balance / Combination

  • Red and Black was chosen as the theme color since these color's represents a professional look for the website.
  • There is a well balanced decent contrast for the page.
  • Color combination of the Navigation bar and Hover effects were also selected considering the theme color of the page.
  • Font colors were also set matching the background of each page.
  • Suitable font color and background was given to the mouse over hover effect of Group Members' Details.

Gestalt Principles for grouping

    Similarity

  • Our brain uses similarity to distinguish between objects who may lay adjacent to or overlap with each other based upon their visual texture
  • Smaller elements are assembled in a compostion.
  • All the contents in the quiz were made with a same backgorund and hidden properties were given using CSS to some elements.

    • Proximity

  • The Gestalt law of proximity states that "objects or shapes that are close to one another appear to form groups".
  • Texts are followed by Images.
  • All the pages and contents were well balanced and well-formed.

    • Closure

  • The Quiz form is developed as a sperate content and users can easily identify where every single elements exists.

    • Good Form

  • The principle of good form refers to the tendency to group together forms of similar shape, pattern, color etc.
  • All the tasks given (Main page and the Quiz) are well grouped
