Information Page Design

Accessibility

  • Animation of the names list in presentation was made very slow to be more readable for users.
  • Products of the store are indicated very clearly so that the user can easily recognise what he or she wants.
  • Products description is vey thorough so the user can have more insights over the product
  • Instant total price display is given to make the store more efficient
  • Small pop E-bill is given to the user for safety issues.
  • A small button is given to each and every page to increase/decrease the foint size of the web page to be more readable for the user.

Typhography

  • Fonts like “PT Serif”, “Chalkduster”, “Comic sans”, “Goudy Bookletter 1911” were used for the Personal Details page and for the Store page..
  • Topics and normal paragraph/wordings were given different font sizes because to divide and be more readable for user.
  • Used "Arial" font for each and every topics in pages made by me to have a theme.
  • Used font colors that mathces with the page background to be more visible to users.

Color Balance / Combination

  • Gradient effect with red and black colours was selected as the background theme to give more attractive look for the website.
  • Navigation bar colors are well balanced and matches with background theme of the website .
  • Store page products table colors are balanced and matches with the background theme of the website.
  • Website theme colours are used in the presentation page as well to make the opening of the website look well.
  • Personal details and store page font colours are well combined to be more visible

Contrast

  • Color contrast in the website is very effective
  • Product indicators in store page have high contrast images than text colors
  • Text in the presentation page have the visibility to read because of high contrast texts

Gestalt Principles for grouping

    Similarity

  • Our brain uses similarity to distinguish between objects who may lay adjacent to or overlap with each other based upon their visual texture
  • Store page contents are made with a same background and CSS stylesheets gave hiden properties to elements

    • Proximity

  • The Gestalt law of proximity states that "objects or shapes that are close to one another appear to form groups".
  • Images have text as discriptions.
  • Content of the page is well balanced and well formed

    • Closure

  • Names of the group members in the presentation page is moved together to give more closure for users.

    • Good Form

  • The principle of good form refers to the tendency to group together forms of similar shape, pattern, color etc.
